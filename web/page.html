HexaHive

What is HexaHive?

HexaHive is a educational calculator where one can upload an image and the most prominent colors are recognized as hexadecimal notation. For example #FF0000 for "red". Hexadecimal is a system ranging from 0-9, A-F representating colors. With HexaHive, the colors generated from the image will convert from hexadecimal to binary.
